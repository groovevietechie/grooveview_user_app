# GrooveVie Lounge - Complete Operations & Customer App Overview

## Executive Summary

**GrooveVie** is a comprehensive digital platform for lounge and hospitality businesses that enables customers to browse menus, order food and drinks, book services, and make payments seamlessly through their mobile devices via QR code scanning. The platform eliminates the need for physical menus and streamlines the entire customer experience from browsing to payment.

---

## 🎯 Core Concept

GrooveVie transforms traditional lounge operations into a modern, contactless digital experience where:
- Customers scan a QR code to access the business menu
- Browse food, drinks, and services on their mobile device
- Place orders directly from their table, room, or for home delivery
- Track orders in real-time
- Make payments via multiple methods
- Earn and redeem loyalty rewards automatically

**No app download required** - Everything works through the mobile web browser.

### 5. **Reward Tokens System** 💰

#### How It Works

**Earning Tokens**
- Customers earn **2% tokens** on every completed order
- Example: ₦10,000 order = ₦200 tokens earned
- Tokens automatically credited when order is marked as "served" and "paid"
- Available across all linked devices instantly

**Using Tokens**
- Use tokens to pay for any order (full or partial payment)
- Toggle "Use Tokens" at checkout
- Slider to adjust token amount
- Real-time calculation shows savings
- Remaining balance updated immediately

**Token Features**
- 1 Token = ₦1 Naira (direct currency value)
- No expiration (configurable)
- Works across all devices
- Visible in Device Sync Modal
- Transparent balance tracking
- Audit trail for all transactions

**Benefits**
- Encourages repeat customers
- Increases customer loyalty
- Automatic reward system
- No manual management needed
- Built-in analytics for businesses

### 6. **Device Sync & Multi-Device Experience** 🔄

#### The Problem Solved
Customers often use multiple devices (personal phone, work phone, tablet) and want their order history and rewards accessible everywhere.

#### How It Works

**First Device Setup**
1. Customer scans QR code and browses menu
2. Places first order
3. Clicks "Sync Devices" button
4. Creates customer profile
5. Receives unique 6-digit passcode (e.g., "123 456")

**Linking Additional Devices**
1. Customer scans QR code on second device
2. Clicks "Sync Devices"
3. Selects "I Have a Passcode"
4. Enters 6-digit code
5. Device instantly linked to profile

**What Syncs Across Devices**
- Order history
- Reward token balance
- Service bookings
- Customer preferences
- Activity history
- Cart items (optional)

**Device Management**
- View all linked devices
- See last active timestamp
- Unlink devices anytime
- Regenerate passcode if needed
- Current device clearly marked

**Security Features**
- Unique 6-digit passcode per customer
- Device fingerprinting
- Activity tracking
- Unlink suspicious devices
- No password required

## 🎯 Customer Journey Flow

### Scenario 1: First-Time Dine-In Customer

1. **Arrival**: Customer sits at table in lounge
2. **Scan**: Scans QR code on table
3. **Browse**: Opens menu on phone, browses food and drinks
4. **Customize**: Selects items, customizes options (size, toppings)
5. **Cart**: Adds items to cart with special notes
6. **Checkout**: Enters table number, chooses payment method
7. **Order**: Places order
8. **Track**: Watches order status update in real-time
9. **Receive**: Waiter delivers order to table
10. **Pay**: Pays via chosen method (cash/POS/transfer)
11. **Earn**: Automatically earns 2% tokens
12. **Sync**: Creates profile to sync devices and track rewards

### Scenario 2: Returning Customer with Tokens

1. **Arrival**: Customer returns to lounge
2. **Scan**: Scans QR code
3. **Sync**: Device automatically recognizes customer profile
4. **Browse**: Sees token balance (₦500 available)
5. **Order**: Adds items worth ₦2,000
6. **Checkout**: Toggles "Use Tokens"
7. **Apply**: Uses ₦500 tokens, pays ₦1,500 cash
8. **Save**: Saves ₦500 on order
9. **Earn**: Earns ₦40 new tokens (2% of ₦2,000)
10. **Repeat**: Encouraged to return and use more tokens

### Scenario 3: Service Booking Customer

1. **Browse**: Customer scans QR code
2. **Services**: Clicks "Services" tab
3. **Select**: Chooses "Party Booking"
4. **Options**: Adds drinks, food, DJ, decorations
5. **Details**: Fills booking form (date, time, guest count)
6. **Review**: Reviews total cost and options
7. **Book**: Submits booking request
8. **Confirm**: Receives booking confirmation
9. **Payment**: Makes advance payment
10. **Event**: Attends event, enjoys services

### Scenario 4: Home Delivery Customer

1. **Browse**: Customer scans QR code from home
2. **Order**: Adds food items to cart
3. **Checkout**: Selects "Home Delivery"
4. **Address**: Enters full delivery address and phone
5. **Payment**: Proceeds to bank transfer payment
6. **Transfer**: Makes payment to business account
7. **Proof**: Uploads payment screenshot
8. **Confirm**: Order confirmed after payment verification
9. **Track**: Tracks delivery in real-time
10. **Receive**: Receives order at home
